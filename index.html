At What Temperature can I Store my Products?

At Cold Storage, we have temperature controlled warehouse for both Freezer and chiller products, with their temperature ranging from -18 to +20 Degree Celsius.

A temperature controlled cold storage is the key to preserve food and fresh produce that requires to maintain customised climatic conditions such as temperature according to the product requirement. At Cold Storage we have a robust refrigeration system and technology to maintain the condition of the products.

Can you control the storage room size based on the storage temperature I choose?

Definitely. Please contact us to discover if a leased storage area will best serve your needs. Store your perishables at -18 degrees or other products at +20 degrees and let us manage the size of the space, or, go with one of our leased warehouses to meet unique requirements and gain full control over the amount of space required.

At Cold Storage we have the flexibility to tailor make solutions to the needs of individual clients, offering even a minimum of 1 pallet to even a full dedicated chamber.

What kinds of products can i store at your warehouse

We are currently storing various products like Pharma, fresh juices, seafood, meat, poultry, momos, dim-sums, dairy products (cheese, yoghurt, butter, whipped cream, ice-cream), green peas, sweet corn, soya bean based products, potato based frozen products, liquid egg, chocolates, cakes and bakery, fruits(banana, apple, oranges, etc.), dry fruits, dates, spices, imported flowers, seeds & bulbs, vegetables (potatoes, onion, carrot, etc.) and beverages etc.
